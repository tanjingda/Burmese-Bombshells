BURMESE BOMBSHELLS

ABOUT US

Burmese Bombshells is an indie group from Singapore comprising Meg-Mel Dean (vocals), Tan Jing Da (guitar), Felix Galistan (bass), Ryan Chua (drums) and Ethan Chan (keys/guitar).

The band was formed in 2015 with the sole purpose of winning a high school talent show and performed “Bad Example” – the first song we ever wrote. We took second place to a magician. Nonetheless, that moment catalysed an adrenaline rush that has sustained itself ever since, manifesting in dozens of school festivals, charity concerts and weddings along the way. In 2019, Burmese Bombshells produced an eponymous debut EP in a study, releasing music on international platforms for the first time.

OUR PROCESS

As university students, the band members only meet during the winter and summer holidays. Once they come together, hours are spent trying to come up with a creative yet catchy melody to go along with lyrics that hold personal meaning to the writer of the song. Eventually, the structure of the song gets completed and all that's left to do is to record the different parts and edit it to perfection.
